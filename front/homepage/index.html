Splitting principal and interest

rDai (redeemable DAI) allows you to invest your money in a risk free* interest generating pool, while holding on to the amount you invested.
You decide who benefits from the generated interest: yourself, a community fund, a charity, or a dApp you are using.
rDAI is a fungible ERC20 token, meaning you can send any amount to any user, and they can always redeem it 1:1 for DAI

Use Cases

  • For users

    Keep your rDAI safe in cold storage, and keep the interest in a hot wallet for everyday spending.

  • For Communities

    Exchange some DAI for rDAI, and set a community DAO as your beneficiary. Transact freely within the community members, and your DAI will always be working for the community.

  • For Buidlers

    Monetize your deposits by using rDAI in your dApp instead of simple DAI. While using your app, users will grant you their accrued interest, with no friction.

  • For DeFi

    Use rDAI as a financial primitive for new and exciting use cases, bringing easy monetization while maintaining frictionless ERC20 transfer capabilities.
